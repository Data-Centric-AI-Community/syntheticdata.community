Tabular data

Learn how to generate synthetic tabular data while leveraging the GAN-based model from the research to generate high-fidelity synthetic data in a data driven-approach.

You can find Tensorflow 2 implementations for some of the major architectures such as: Vanilla GAN, WGAN or WCGAN.

Time-series data

Sequential data can be found pretty much anywhere: from weather forecasted, exchange rates, to our mobility.

Learn how to capture dataset features distributions within each time-point with data-driven approaches such as: TimeGAN, CGAN or RCGAN.
